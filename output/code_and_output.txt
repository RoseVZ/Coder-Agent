```python
# Python program to calculate the first 10,000 terms of the series 
# and multiply the total by 4

total = 0.0
for i in range(10000):
    term = (-1)**i / (2 * i + 1)  # Calculate the term: 1/(2*i + 1) and alternate signs
    total += term

result = total * 4  # Multiply by 4 as per the problem statement
print(result)
```
